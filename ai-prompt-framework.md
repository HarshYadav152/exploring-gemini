### The `PTCF` framework
1. P - Persona (who you want to AI to be)
2. T - Task (what the AI should do)
3. C - Context (backgound information AI needs)
4. F - Format (how you want the output structure)

### advance prompting techniques
1. Zero Shot (without example)
2. One Shot (with one example)
3. Few Shot (2 to 10 example)

### Chain of Thoughts
Ask the AI to think step by step

### Beta Prompting
rewriting the prompt through AI model itself
